# 🐟 Fishonary

Build your own fish dictionary

Discover, log, and share your fish catches on the go! Fishonary helps you track fish locations and build your own fishing journey dictionary.

---

## 🚀 Features

Built for adventurers, hobbyists, and ocean lovers, Fishonary brings entertainment and travel together in one fun and easy-to-use app.

- Capture and share moments from your fishing trips
- Map your catches and view your personal fishing road map
- See the Caught Fish photo, Date, and GPS coordinates with your memory
- Multi-language support (e.g., English, Korean, Chinese, Japanese...)
